# 📚 Advanced Mathematics Notes & Solutions

This repository is a long-term project documenting my journey through a collection of **advanced mathematics texts**. For each book, I work through a substantial portion of the exercises and write **rigorous, well-structured solutions in LaTeX**.

The goal is twofold:

* Develop a deep, working understanding of advanced mathematics
* Build a high-quality, self-contained archive of solved problems and notes

---

## ✨ What You'll Find

* 📖 **Book-specific directories** containing:

  * LaTeX source files
  * Compiled PDFs (when available)
  * Structured solutions to exercises

* 🧠 Emphasis on:

  * Clear reasoning and complete proofs
  * Concise but precise explanations
  * Mathematical maturity over hand-wavy intuition

## 🧾 Philosophy

Most solutions are written from scratch after attempting problems independently. When external ideas are used, they are internalized and rewritten in my own words and structure.

This repository is not meant to be a shortcut or answer key-it is a **record of learning through effort**.
